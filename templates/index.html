Upload a Junk Image for Volume Estimation

Upload an image of junk items to receive an estimate of their volume.

Supported formats: JPG, PNG, HEIF, HEIC

About the Junk Volume Estimator

This tool uses computer vision to:

  • Detect different types of junk items in your image
  • Estimate the volume of each detected item
  • Calculate the total volume of all items
  • Provide a range of possible volumes based on confidence

Supported junk categories include trash bags, wooden debris, construction debris, garbage, and pipes.
